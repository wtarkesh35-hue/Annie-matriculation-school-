Our Story

Excellence in Education
Since 2010

Annie Matriculation Higher Secondary School was established in 2010 in the heart of Madanapuram, Chennai, with a singular purpose — to provide quality, value-based education to every child.

Affiliated to the Tamil Nadu Matriculation Board, we offer a complete educational journey from Class I through Class XII — Primary, Middle, Secondary, and Higher Secondary — all under one roof.

Guided by our motto of Truth, Love and Wisdom, we have grown into one of the most trusted and respected institutions in the Madanapuram region, consistently delivering 100% board results and producing district-level toppers.
